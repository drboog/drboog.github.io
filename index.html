Bio

I am a Ph.D. student (Fall 2018 - present) co-advised by Prof. Jinhui Xu and Prof. Changyou Chen. I received my B.E. degree from Zhejiang University.
I'm broadly interested in machine learning and deep learning.
Currently, I'm using deep learning methods to construct and learn 'better' kernels, and utilizing them in deep generative models, meta-learning, energy-based models, etc.
I`m also interested in Wasserstein Gradient Flow and Neural Tangent Kernel.
I worked as a Research Intern with Chunyuan Li (Microsoft Research, Summer 2020), Ruiyi Zhang (Adobe Research, Summer 2021).
