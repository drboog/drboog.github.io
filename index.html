Bio

I will join Adobe Research as a Research Scientist.

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate (Fall 2018 - Spring 2023) co-advised by Prof. Jinhui Xu and Prof. Changyou Chen. I received my B.E. degree from Zhejiang University.
I'm broadly interested in machine learning and deep learning. Currently I focus on generative models especially multi-modal generative models.
I worked as a Research Intern with Chunyuan Li (Microsoft), Ruiyi Zhang (Adobe), Bingchen Liu (ByteDance).

  1. Shifted Diffusion for Text-to-image Generation
    (code and pre-trained models will be available soon)
    Yufan Zhou, Bingchen Liu, Yizhe Zhu, Xiao Yang, Changyou Chen, Jinhui Xu.
