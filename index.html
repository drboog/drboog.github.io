Bio

I am a Ph.D. student (Fall 2018 - present) co-advised by Prof. Jinhui Xu and Prof. Changyou Chen. I got my bachelor's degree from Zhejiang University.
My research focuses on machine learning and deep learning.
Currently, I'm using deep learning methods to learn 'better' kernels, and utilizing them in deep generative models, meta-learning, etc.
I`m also interested in Wasserstein Gradient Flow and Neural Tangent Kernel.
